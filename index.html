Changing the NonLinear Optics Paradigm with Metal-Organic Framework (MOF) Technology

MAQI develops custom-designed optical crystals using advanced metal-organic framework (MOF) structures to transform the field of optics. Our MOF-based materials deliver superior performance compared to traditional off-the-shelf nonlinear crystals, enabling new capabilities for a wide range of optical applications. Through the integration of computational physics, materials science, and cutting-edge optics, we provide innovative solutions that push the boundaries of nonlinear optical technologies.

Technology: Advancing Custom-Made Nonlinear Optical Crystals

Fundamental research on metal-organic framework (MOF) crystals for advanced optics at the Universidad de Santiago de Chile led to breakthroughs in crystal fabrication, enabling the development of MIRO-101, the first bulk MOF crystal designed for high-efficiency optical frequency conversion. This achievement marked a major step toward integrating MOFs into scalable nonlinear photonics and expanding the performance frontier of nonlinear optical crystals.

MAQI now delivers custom-made advanced solutions for the photonics market, offering tailored MOF-based materials engineered to meet the demands of next-generation optical technologies.

Our MOF Optical Crystals

Our custom-made nonlinear crystals are based on MOFs: We design them, grow them and characterise them.

Why using MOFs for optics?

Traditional nonlinear crystals such as KTP, BBO, and LN have reached their performance limits, showing constraints in damage thresholds, effective SHG nonlinear coefficients across key wavelengths, and overall thermal and mechanical stability.

We design and develop next-generation nonlinear optical crystals based on metal-organic frameworks (MOFs), a breakthrough class of materials that overcome the limitations of conventional crystals like KDP, KTP, BBO, and LN. Our MOF-based crystals combine exceptional stability, high resistance to optical damage, and tunable optical properties with an energy-efficient and cost-effective manufacturing process—positioning them to transform the future of photonics.

We are actively seeking new technical validation partners. If you are exploring advanced nonlinear optical solutions, reach out to discuss how our MOF-based crystals can elevate your project.

About us

MAQI was founded as a university spin-off in 2023, with a dedicated team of physicists, chemists and engineers with combined expertise in material science and advanced photonic technologies. Our team is committed to driving photonics innovations by offering state-of-the-art solutions through custom-designed optical crystals based on MOFs that meet the specific needs of the photonics industry in both industrial and academic sectors. We are proud to pioneer the introduction of MOF technology in the global photonics industry for applications in telecommunications, sensing and computing.
